Great design combined with a solid idea is a winning recipe for creating truly exceptional products. The first piece of the puzzle is function - serving a purpose and solving a problem in a clever way. This means guiding the user, surfacing complexity only when necessary, combining insights with a strong opinion, and building systems that support and do not restrain scalability.
The second part is all about emotional resonance - turning a product from something they use into something they love. This creates trust, loyalty, and an emotional connection that forms a true Moat. In practice, this means delightful microinteractions, visual details, personalized onboardings, positive reinforcement and storytelling.
